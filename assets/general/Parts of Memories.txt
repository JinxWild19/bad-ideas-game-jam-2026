###Frostine Memory###
“Oh. Hello there little one. Where did you come from?” It was a cat at her feet. She bent down to pick up the hefty beast. It was a bigger cat then ones she typically saw in the village. It had light colored fur like blue snow with dark marks and circles dotted around its extremely long body. Something in her mind clicked. Just a little while holding the cat. A memory of lightning and a massive storm. A woman in a small...studio. As Melisa stood she could feel the cat purring.
“What is going on, Frostine?” Melisa said both to the cat and to no one.
Melisa shook her head and looked down at the cat in her hands.
“Oh aren’t you a cute one. I’d love to stick around and play with you but I must be off.” She went to put the cat down but it crawled up and set its self across her shoulders. “I guess you can stay…” She wasn’t sure why the cat was clinging to her but cats were funny like that sometimes.


###Dragon Memory Bare Skin###
"How have you been, Llirrtedi? Did you miss me?" Holly says, arms wide open for a hug. Not thinking, Melisa runs up and hugs Holly. It's when Melisa pulls away and sees the smirk on Holly and Damar's faces that she realizes she missed something. 
The rider was holding a cloak and trousers.

###Bird Fight Part 1###
From above, a flock of birds dove down, attempting to land in the wagon, only to bounce off an invisible barrier. The birds that bounced off landed on the ground turning into men in robes. Seemingly unperturbed by the impact, they rolled and produced bows and arrows and began to shoot at the fleeing wagon.
